Developer Blogs

October 2020 - The beginning

This is the month that started the whole process, we are set to a journey to complete greatness!

We knew creating this application would be a considerable challenge over the months, however, with great courage, ambition and talent we knew this can be possible too!

October is the month we rendezvous to ensure we as a company have full control of the product we are creating together, to ensure this happens we have assigned roles that we would specialise and confidently be productive in.

This enabled us to focus each aspect of the product with great attention to detail and efficiency.

From there we forumalted what is going to be this application? how are our customers going to have fun with it?

All of these questions must be answered so that our game is successful, all of these answers can be solved through our developement method which is AGILE!

Through iterations of UML case diagrams, user inter face design and conducting a report of how we are going to improve the quality of our software, this has enabled us to keep a certain high standard on what we want to achieve as we want to ensure the product we are creating looks and functions on what we have envisioned together

Before even proceeding to create the GUI of the application, we have decided to research various libraries and frameworks that could boost efficiency creating the application as Java alone is the best language to programme games.

The most fun step is when we were designing the website, we enjoyed developing an experience that would attract our target customer,

After countless amount of testing and interations of UML cases, we have decided to create two applications. One for creating the user experience and the other to test functionality.

This would enable us to create to test cases, one for the user interface and one for simulating functionality

The main challenge facing us is how do we show cards that are being compared and how do we add players to ensure multiple users can play?

These are questions that will be answered soon to ensure completion of our project!

The main foundations of our website is now complete, and we look forward to link it with a database and our game so users are able to download the game, create their account and see various metrics!

Bahman one of our main software developers has figured a way to ensure how we can deal the cards between the players, when it happens it looks like a magic moment through our coding history

From this point our confidence of developing a game from scratch is starting to rise than ever

Abbas our software developer has added more functionality to the game to improve the overall scope of the user experience

Some of this include adding a setting function and a game rules area to ensure every user understand the capability of our game.

As you enter in to our game you will also isanely beautiful music that will improve your overall experience of your game too!

This function is the main tool that will enable the whole game to be functional as a whole!

As a group we have discovered that having a game engine is more vital than ever, however, this isnt going to stop us as we have increased our efforts by 500%.

Overall it could be said that we have declared war against our own game but also game engines as we have proof that it could be done without one

The gramifications of these revelations could possibly be dangerous to our deadline however, beneficial to our knowledge retention stand point
